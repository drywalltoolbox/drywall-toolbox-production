Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer free shipping on all orders over $500. For orders under $500, standard shipping is $49.99. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

Do you offer warranties on your products?

All products come with manufacturer warranties. Warranty periods vary by brand and product. Please contact us for specific warranty information.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, we accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in original packaging. Contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer competitive pricing for bulk orders. Contact our sales team at sales@drywalltoolbox.com for a custom quote.
